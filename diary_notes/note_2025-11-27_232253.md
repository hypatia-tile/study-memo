## Difference between Deploy, Release and Launch

* References:
  - [https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ant-murphy_deploy-vs-release-vs-launch-deploy-activity-7307551321702703104-tre_]

Short answer:
- Deploy: putting new code into an environment (e.g., staging, production).
- Release: making new change/functionality available to users.
- Launch: informing the market about specific features and changes.
